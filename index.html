Cross River State

Cross River State is located in the South-South geopolitical zone of Nigeria. Named after the Cross River, the state was created on 27 May 1967 with Calabar as its capital city. It shares borders with Benue, Ebonyi, Abia, Akwa Ibom, and Cameroon.
